Combo backtest

Searches combinations that freely mix EVERY indicator column (each compared to its own trailing rolling median) together with every price-action signal - ALL must agree on direction on the same candle - and backtests each one for real PnL the same realistic way as a single signal (entry at next open, stop/target bracket, risk-based sizing, fees). Most combinations naturally get zero/few fires since requiring several conditions to align exactly is rare; only profitable combinations are shown below, ranked by total PnL so the biggest profit/capital-growth combination is always row 1.
